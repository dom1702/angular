Book a driving lesson via the menu item on the left hand menu. Preselect your desired driving lesson. There are four input fields to be considered. Choose your desired driving lesson in the Type field. Chose your desired vehicle in the Vehicle field. If you wish to drive several hours comined, select it in the Length field. Finally find your date in the Date field. By hitting the pick button you see a scheduler for your current week. Red blocks are blocked timeslots for your desired driving lesson. White timeslots are outside of the common business hours for your driving school. By hitting an free timeslot, the date will be selected for your driving lesson.
Communicate with other persons in Drima via the chat. You can find the chat bar by hitting the upper right chat symbol. When the chat bar is opened, you can add a friend via the displayed input field. Type in the name of a user. A list of found users appears. Find the right user and pick the button 'add' left to the users name. When the chat bar is opened you can add a friend to your blocked persons list by hitting his name in the list. The chat dialog appears. On the upper right there is a menu point. Choose 'block user'
